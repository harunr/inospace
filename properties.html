Bofors Business Park

78 Bofors Circle, Epping Industria, Cape Town
360° enquire

Situated off Bofors Circle and minutes from Cape Town’s three national roads Hewetts is the ideal choice for a company looking to situate themselves in the heart of Cape Town with fantastic transport links, providing easy access across the city.

About this park

The Park has become a home to vehicle suppliers and light manufacturers.

Life in the park

Flexit Engineering Supplies, a growing group focused on vehicle supplies, is joined by Speedy Exhausts in their recently refurbished retail showroom. Excell Screen Printers and Signature Bottle Printers are both rapidly growing promotional manufacturing suppliers. Bofors Business Park is part of Epping’s most innovative business centre groups where design, client facilities and a bustling community of successful businesses combine to provide a truly unique environment.

Area Guide

Epping Industria is one of Cape Town’s most central business locations and home to established companies involved in textiles, automotive, warehousing, printing, logistics, construction and food processing. With 24-hour security, the acclaimed Epping City Improvement District (ECID) has contributed to low crime rates, clean roads and impressive emergency and traffic management. Epping has wide roads with excellent access to major arterial roads (N7, N1, N2, M5 and the R300) as well as most forms of public transport including rail, taxi and bus.

Types of space
