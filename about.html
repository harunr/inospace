Who We Are

A leading Southern African owner and manager of business centres.

Inospace is a specialised South African commercial property fund that acquires, owns and manages branded multi-tenant, multi-use business parks and centres. We focus on leased space for growing and mid-sized companies and provide innovative services, products and facilities.

Nicholas van Eeden

Chief Executive Officer

Nick (B.Com, Financial Management) was appointed to the Board in 2016 and took on the role as CEO. He has considerable experience in commercial property, having been a portfolio manager and investment manager at a multi-billion Rand property fund. Nick joined Inospace from Investec Bank’s property finance team where he was a senior consultant. Neville Bear

Chief Operating Officer

Neville (Accis, Chartered Secretary) was appointed as COO to manage our group operations from Johannesburg. He leads Inospace’s highly specialised operations platform that manages the day-to-day operation of our properties. As the former COO of Propertuity (owners of Johannesburg’s Maboneng Prencinct) and the Commercial Property Director of the Alliance Group, Neville brings two decades of commercial property experience to our team.

Ashraf Mohammed

Independent Non-Executive Director

Ashraf (B.Com, Finance and Accounts) was appointed as Chairman and non-executive Director of Inospace in 2016. He has broad experience in property funds, having been the CEO of Ascension Properties that was listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange. After the company was sold to Rebosis Property Fund Ashraf became Chief Investment Officer of Pareto Ltd. He is the current executive Director of the Sycamore Fund and leads the Inospace board with rectitude and discipline.

The Right strategy

Our business model is underpinned by 6 strategic pillars

  • The Right Focus

    InoSpace has a simple business model and a focused strategy. We invest in multi-building, multi-tenant office and industrial properties typically measuring 5,000 – 30,000 sqm. We are not a developer but a transformer of properties that offers attractive, low-risk, income-led returns and capital growth in all market conditions.

  • The Right Properties

    We identify, transform and assets and integrate them into our network of sites under the Inospace brand. Through repositioning projects we create modernised, higher quality, well designed spaces that offer value adding facilities. We constantly upgrade our existing properties through adapting to client requirements and evolving trends.

  • The Right Clients

    We regard those who occupy our spaces as our clients and we continually build our understanding of their needs. We target the right clients for the right properties and build long term relationships by offering unique lease products and foster long term relationships through community development, networking, inter-trading and communication.

  • The Right People

    We invest in human talent to deliver our strategy of a scalable business that is underpinned by operational excellence. We employ quality people with skills reflecting all aspects of real estate ownership including asset management, redevelopment, investment, marketing and financing. Skilled expertise, market knowledge, aligned goals and a dynamic innovative culture allows us to build a robust sustainable business.

  • The Right Brand

    We are focused on building a client-centric brand that encourages long-term advocacy, builds financial value and drives income. It is key for InoSpace to highlight our differentiated offerings and to have a stand-out brand that creates barriers to entry and allows us to expand into new geographic markets.

  • The Right Controls

    Our entrepreneurial flair is strengthened by rigorous corporate governance. Our prudent risk management approach, tight financial controls and sound corporate structure underpins our business. Real estate is capital intensive and we consider all possible outcomes at a project, asset and corporate level.
